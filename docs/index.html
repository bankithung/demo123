Heritage & Engineering Marvels

Kachari legacy · living hydro-engineering
1
Rich Kachari (Dimasa) history10th century
Dimapur was the ancient Kachari capital — megalithic ruins dating to the 10th century survive at its heart. A powerful, authentic branding platform for the circuit.
2
Ancient ponds — engineering that still worksAll-season
The historic waterbodies neither dry up in winter nor flood in the monsoon — superior early hydro-engineering, ideal for education, wellness and leisure tourism.
